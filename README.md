# RM-Tools

A multi-purpose calculator for midwives

Clinical Tools for Registered Midwives

- RM tools was built because I was tired of not having a centralized place to access commonly used, simple clinical tools in one place. These tools are mostly relevant to clinical midwifery or obstetric practice in a Canadian setting, but some of them may be universally useful. It's designed to be quick and easy to use while in prenatal appointments, at a birth, or on the go while out doing community care. Live testing environment for now lives at: zenobiavayne.github.io/RM-Tools

Features:
-Prenatal Tools - Edd Calculator - calculate from LMP, GA based on Ultrasound, or ART transfer date. - Care Planner - Generate a prenatal care timeline based on provincial guidelines. I mostly wanted this because figuring out when to send someone for SIPS, NIPS, FTS, or eFTS is painfully tedious to do by hand or using available tools. It also includes other commonly used date windows for prenatal tests or interventions. Can be localized to BC, AB, or ON for now.
-Newborn Tools
-Age in Hours
-Percentiles - coming soon
-Bili nomogram link - look, hyperbili is what you should be using for your bili testing, not my pale imitation copy of their tool, so i've just included a link to hyperbili.com.

Privacy
All calculations are performed locally on device. There is no functionality that stores patient data or transmits it.

Disclaimer
This is a homebrew tool designed to support, not replace, clinical judgement. All results should be interpreted in the context of the clinical picutre. Provincial screening guidelines are subject to change or may not be relevant to your clinical use case. Always verify against current local standards and other validated tools.
